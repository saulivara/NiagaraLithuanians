Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the community?

Anyone interested in Lithuanian culture is welcome to join! Simply attend one of our events or contact us through the form above. Membership is free and open to all.

Do I need to speak Lithuanian?

Not at all! While we offer Lithuanian language classes, many of our members are learning the language. We conduct most activities in English with Lithuanian cultural elements.

What events do you host?

We host cultural festivals, language classes, folk art workshops, music nights, and traditional holiday celebrations throughout the year.

Is there a membership fee?

No membership fee is required! We are a volunteer-run community organization. Some events may have small fees to cover materials or venue costs.

Can I volunteer with the community?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers for events, language classes, cultural programs, and community outreach. Contact us to learn about current opportunities.

Do you have programs for children?

Yes! We offer Lithuanian language classes for children, cultural workshops, and family-friendly events that help kids connect with their heritage.
