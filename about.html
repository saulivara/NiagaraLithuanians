Founded by local Lithuanians

We started as neighbours and friends who missed the tastes and traditions of home. Over time, a simple idea took root: share what we love about Lithuania with the people around us here in Niagara.

You’ll find us baking fresh Lithuanian bread, brewing gira (our beloved rye drink), and gathering for Christmas customs that fill the table with warmth and the room with song. Some of us were born in Lithuania, some here—but we all carry the same spirit of hospitality.

If you’re curious about Lithuanian culture—or just hungry for good bread—you’re welcome to join us at our events, try a recipe, or simply say hello.
