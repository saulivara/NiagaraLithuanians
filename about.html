About Our Community

Discover the rich history and vibrant culture of Lithuanian-Canadians in the Niagara region

Our Story

The Niagara Lithuanians community was founded in 1998 by a group of Lithuanian immigrants and their descendants who settled in the beautiful Niagara region of Ontario, Canada. What began as informal gatherings to celebrate Lithuanian holidays and traditions has grown into a vibrant, close-knit community of over 150 families.

Our community is built on the foundation of preserving and sharing Lithuanian culture, language, and traditions while embracing our Canadian identity. We believe that cultural diversity enriches our society and that maintaining our heritage helps us understand who we are and where we come from.

Today, we continue to grow and evolve, welcoming new members from all backgrounds who share our passion for Lithuanian culture and community spirit. Whether you're a first-generation immigrant, a descendant of Lithuanian heritage, or simply someone interested in learning about our culture, you'll find a warm welcome in our community.
